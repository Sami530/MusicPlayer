Vivian Fernandes, better known by his stage name Divine, is an Indian rapper from Mumbai, Maharashtra. He started to gain popularity after the release of his song "Yeh Mera Bombay". He broke through with the release of his song "Mere Gully Mein" featuring fellow Mumbai rapper Naezy.

Nusrat Fateh Ali Khan was a Pakistani vocalist, musician, composer and music director, primarily a singer of qawwali, a form of Sufi devotional music.

Arijit Singh is an Indian singer and music composer. He sings predominantly in Hindi and Bengali, but has also performed in various other Indian languages. He is the recipient of a National Award and a record six Filmfare Awards
